Quick Start Guide

Master Your Layouts

Get from a folder of photos to a perfectly printed sheet in under 60 seconds.

01

Define Your Paper

Click "Paper Style" to set your page dimensions and grid matrix. You can save these as templates for future use (e.g., "A4 5x5 Grid" or "4x6 Portrait").

02

Import Photos

Click "Add Photos" to load your high-resolution image files into the catalog. Don't worry about cropping yet—you can adjust that later.

03

Stamp the Grid

Select a photo from the catalog, choose an Apply Range (Cell, Row, or After), and click the grid to fill it. Use "Clear" mode to remove mistakes just as easily.

CLICK!
04

Print Direct

Click "Print Direct." Select your printer and hardware options (like paper quality) in the native Windows dialog. The app will send the original pixel data for maximum sharpness.
