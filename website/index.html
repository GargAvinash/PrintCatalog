Why GDI Printing?

PrintCatalog uses the native Windows GDI (Graphics Device Interface) pipeline to communicate directly with your printer hardware, ensuring exact sizing and resolution.

  • Pixel Perfect: No intermediate downscaling or compression.
  • Hardware Direct: Native DEVMODE support for custom paper sizes.
  • Interpolation: Professional-grade HALFTONE scaling for sharp results.
